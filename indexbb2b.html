What are research peptides, and how are they used?

Research peptides are chemical compounds designed strictly for scientific and laboratory research. They are not intended for human or veterinary use and are typically used in preclinical experiments, academic studies, or product development testing.

Are your peptides tested for purity?

Yes. All Westbridge Research peptides undergo independent third-party testing to verify purity, consistency, and quality. We provide Certificates of Analysis (COAs) with each batch for complete transparency.

Are your products legal to purchase internationally?

Our products are legal to purchase in both the United States and Canada aswell as Europe and the UK for research use only. It is the buyers responsibility to ensure compliance with local, state, and provincial regulations.

Who can order from Westbridge Research?

We sell only to qualified researchers, laboratories, and institutions. Orders from individuals for personal or recreational use will not be accepted. You must be 18 years or older to place an order.
